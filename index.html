About dadada

A publishing
platform built
to last.

DADADA is a lightweight, self-hosted digital publishing platform for artists — built entirely with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JSON. No database, no subscriptions, no algorithmic feed.

Designed with a quiet archival philosophy inspired by Indexhibit, Cargo, Are.na, Monoskop and artist books. Work published on dadada.in is designed to be readable and accessible for decades.

Artists retain all rights. Revenue split is 70 / 30 in favour of the artist for all print and PDF sales.

Contact: publish@dadada.in
